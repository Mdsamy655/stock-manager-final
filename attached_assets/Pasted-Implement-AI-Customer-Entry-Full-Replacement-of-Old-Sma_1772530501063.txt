STEP 3: AI INTELLIGENT PARSING RULES

The system must intelligently detect:

- Customer Name
- Phone Number
- Address

Users may enter data in ANY format and ANY order.

Examples:

- শাহরিয়ার আহমেদ 01622122818 বালাখাল হাজীগঞ্জ
- 01622122818 শাহরিয়ার বালাখাল
- Balakhal Hajiganj 01622122818 Shahriar
- Name: Shahriar Ahmed Phone: 01622122818 Address: Balakhal
- শাহরিয়ার, 01622122818, হাজীগঞ্জ
- ০১৬২২১২২৮১৮ শাহরিয়ার আহমেদ বালাখাল
- Just name phone address without labels
- Address first, then phone, then name

The AI must NOT depend on fixed order.

---

STEP 4: DETECTION LOGIC REQUIREMENTS

1. Phone Detection:
   
   - Detect Bangladeshi numbers (01XXXXXXXXX)
   - Detect Bangla digits (০১XXXXXXXXX)
   - Convert Bangla digits to English automatically.
   - Extract the 11-digit phone number.

2. Name Detection:
   
   - Detect probable human name.
   - Usually 1–3 words.
   - Exclude numeric strings.
   - Prefer words closest to phone number.

3. Address Detection:
   
   - After removing phone and name,
     everything remaining should be treated as address.
   - Support Bangla and English.
   - Support mixed language.

4. If labels like:
   
   - Name:
   - Phone:
   - Address:
     are present, prioritize labeled extraction.
